Popular Destinations

World's best tourist destinations

KERALA

Kerala is popular because of its backwater, beaches, greenery, Kerala cuisines, honeymoon places, handicraft, spices, etc. When you are on Kerala trips, you will understand why it holds such a special place among the various destinations in India for tourism.

JAMMU & KASHMIR

Though the 5 months between March and August are considered favorable for a Jammu and Kashmir holiday, the ideal time would be from March to June when the climate is pleasant.Foreigners only require a valid Indian visa.Kashmir is the northernmost geographical region of the Indian subcontinent.

HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh is one of the top tourist destinations in India for its weather, attractions, activities, and food. Besides, the locals here are very friendly in nature.The best way for a Himachal tour is by road because you can witness the beautiful scenery.

GUJRAT

Gujarat tour packages offer opportunities to embrace the legacy of the exotic culture. Tourists cannot miss savouring the amazing food in Gujarat.Gujarat is located on the western coast of India and has the longest coastline of 1,600 km among all the states in the country.
